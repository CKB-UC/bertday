HOW TO ADD YOUR 32 PHOTOS
==========================

1. Rename your photos exactly:  photo1.jpg, photo2.jpg, photo3.jpg ... photo32.jpg

2. Drag all 32 files into the "images" folder (next to margaret-placeholder.svg).

3. Open index.html in your browser (just double-click it).
   The photos will automatically:
     - appear as a glowing photo strip in the "HAPPY MEMORIES" section
     - blend softly as dimmed backgrounds behind the Hero, Birthday Girls,
       and Heartfelt Message sections

No code edits needed — just drop the files in with the right names!

Want Margaret's or Miry's main photo card to use a real picture instead of
the placeholder? Open index.html, search for "margaret-placeholder.svg" or
"miry-placeholder.svg", and replace it with your filename, e.g. "photo5.jpg".
